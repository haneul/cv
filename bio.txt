Seungyeop Han is a Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science and
Engineering at University of Washington. His research interests are in the
broad area of distributed systems and computer networks, and also include
related topics like security and privacy. He has published papers in the
premier conferences such as SIGCOMM, Ubicomp, NSDI, CCS, Usenix Security, NIPS,
and WWW.
He received KFAS fellowship in computer science (2010-). Prior to studying in 
UW, he worked for Naver as a software engineer for 3 years. He received his 
B.S. (2005) and M.S. (2007) in Computer Science from KAIST.
